Privacy Policy

Better Rail is designed with your right to privacy in mind.

Better Rail does not explicitly collect any usage or analytics information.

Information from Israel Railways

All information related to station & train routes comes directly from Israel Railways.
When you try to find routes in the app, the request is passed directly to Israel Railways, and the sole responsibility processing the information is at theirs.

Please refer to Israel Railways' privacy policy for more information.
